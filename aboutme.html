My thoughts about ROBOTS!!!!

We talked alot about robots and their ethics today. How did your idea of robotics change? What makes you nervous about future robotics?

Robotics sounds like something fun to playwith like a remote controled car or a drone but there are many series of tasks when it comes to building a robot. My idea on robotics change because I thought that everything that had an BOE was a robot,but as I worked with an actual robot I realized that it is way more than just a BOE. For example, it has to be programed to accomplish a task, there are inputs and outputs to a robot, etc.... Here, many things are needed to make up a robot like: BOE shields, Dreadboards, jumper wires, resistors, LED,Piezo, push buttons, etc. This indicates that a robot is not just about having fun but it also requires many part to create something that is fun. In my honest opinion I am excited to see how the actual robot is build from scratch
